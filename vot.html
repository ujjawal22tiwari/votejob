Roadmap & Progress

Our Vision:

To engineer trust in the digital democratic process through a secure, private, and verifiable voting platform.

Phase 1: Foundation & Core Tech (Completed: March - June 2025)

This phase focused on moving from concept to a functional core. We designed the "Attested Claim-Nonce" ZK model, selected our tech stack (Base, Circom, React), and built the fundamental components.

Key Achievements:

  • Architected the core privacy and security model.
  • Developed and compiled the final ZK circuits.
  • Performed the trusted setup to generate all necessary ZKP keys.
  • Deployed the initial verifier and voting smart contracts.
  • All foundational artifacts are on GitHub, ready for integration.

Phase 2: MVP & Public Testnet Launch (Upcoming: July - September 2025)

Our focus is now on full integration, feature completion, and rigorous testing to launch on the Base Goerli testnet.

Primary Goals:

  • Integration: Achieve the first successful end-to-end vote, connecting the frontend UI, ZKP logic, backend services, and live smart contracts.
  • Feature Completion: Implement the full user experience, including the User Profile section, "Track Vote" feature, and the Results Dashboard.
  • Testing & Launch: Conduct intensive internal security reviews and bug fixing, leading to a public testnet launch to gather community feedback.

Phase 3: Mainnet & Future Growth (Q4 2025 & Beyond)

This phase transitions UNI-VOTE from a test product to a live, production-ready platform.

Next Steps:

  • Mainnet Deployment: After a final security audit and performance review, deploy UNI-VOTE to the Base Mainnet.
  • Growth & Evolution: Actively gather user feedback to plan V2 features, such as advanced voting mechanisms and deeper integrations, to continue growing the UNI-VOTE ecosystem.
